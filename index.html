Australia's Biggest Waiting Room

The Royal Flying Doctor Service is one of the largest and most comprehensive aeromedical organisations globally, providing extensive primary health care and 24-hour emergency service to people who live, work, and travel across the 7.69 million square kilometers of Australia.

New RFDS TV Drama

Five years ago the journey began for a new Australian TV drama that depicts the work of the Royal Flying Doctor Service. Produced by Endemol Shine Australia, season 1 is now available on the Australian commercial free-to-air television network Seven Network, and it has played in New Zealand, the USA, Italy and is scheduled in a number of other countries, including Britain, the Netherlands and Sweden.
